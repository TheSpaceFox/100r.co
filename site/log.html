News

This month, we started porting oquonie to uxn. This is a long time coming, but we weren't sure if it was possible to do, and we still had a lot to learn before even thinking of taking it on. Now, we think we are ready. Rek is re-drawing the sprites(Mastodon), and they look amazing.

Rek made a very adorable little Uxn sprite for Potato that comes up when a rom path was mistyped, see it also on the uxn page.
