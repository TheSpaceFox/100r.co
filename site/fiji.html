fiji

In late June, we sailed to Fiji and stayed for 4 months. The sail to Savusavu took 15 days from Opua, it was a slow ride because of contrary winds.

We experienced complete stillness on the ocena, for the first time.

We stayed in Savusavu for a while, before sailing down to Makogai, a former leper colony. Then, we hopped over to leleuvia, to wait for a window to move to Suva. We stayed in a little anchorage far from downtown, a secluded area with moorings. Eventually, we left for an overnight sail to Momi Bay, to use as a stop-over before going to Malolo Lailai. Our last stop in Fiji was Vuda Point Marina, where we stocked up for the passage north to the marshall islands.

Our favorite food there was taro root and passion fruit.
