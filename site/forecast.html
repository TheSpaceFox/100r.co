forecast

Our favourite weather service is Windy, and while underway, we download weather maps(grib files) through the saildocs service with our SAT Phone which we then process with PredictWind. We pick our weather carefully on passage, no sense in taking unnecessary risks. Sailing with a schedule is dangerous.

For information on storms brewing in the Pacific and Atlantic, as well as passage advice in the South Pacific, we enjoy reading MetBob.
