Shipping

Buying stickers. When buying a sticker sheet, select your shipping destination in the dropdown menu.

Be patient. For physical items(sticker sheets), kindly allow for some time for the letter to arrive. We are a small team, and we package and ship each letter ourselves. We are not always near a post office, or mailbox. All letters ship from Western Canada, and can take more than a month to arrive to select international destinations.

Do you offer tracking? We do not provide tracking with purchases because the associated costs for registered mail, we think, are too high. We prioritize low costs over fast delivery.

Check your delivery address. When purchasing stickers, be sure to check the delivery address so that the letter can make its way to you. If the letter returns to us because of an address error, we will have to request additional postage to send it again(we took on that cost in the past, but it adds up)

Busy Doing Nothing paperback book

Busy Doing Nothing is a 218 page long paperback book, written in English.

  • Black and white print
  • 5.83 x 8.27 in / 148 x 210 mm
  • Matte cover
Buy the book

The book is printed on demand. Lulu.com works with printers all over the world, so that most copies are produced locally and travel relatively short distances. It can take 3 to 5 work days to print the book. Since the books are printed, packaged, and shipped by humans, mistakes may happen. If your book arrives in less-than-perfect condition, contact Lulu.com with images of shipping damage or printing errors, and they will send you another copy.

We also released an e-book version.

Busy Doing Nothing is 218 pages long, written in English, and formatted for e-readers. The first version is still available to read online, but doesn't include the newer content and revised text. Buying this book is a way to support our work, we put a lot of work into it, and we hope you like it.
