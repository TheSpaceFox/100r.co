A long time goal aboard Pino has been to replace our Force10 3-burner LPG oven and to replace it with a single burner.

In the winter we got used to cooking with a single induction plate, and we hadn't use the oven in over 3 years, preferring to make food on the stovetop and to solar cook(see {solar cooking}) in the summer.

We re-homed our LPG stove, it has served us well these past 8 years but it is time to build our dream galley.

With the stove gone, we gained a lot of storage space in the galley. We gimballed the single LPG burner, and built drawers and shelves underneath it to store our cast iron cookware and our produce.
